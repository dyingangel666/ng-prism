Flexible button with five visual variants. Designed around the design-system prism palette and exposing the full Angular signal API. Accepts either a label, an icon, or both.

              

                @since0.3.0
                @moduleacme/ui-inputs
                @seeacme-icon-button
                @a11yWAI-ARIA Button Pattern
              


              
Inputs

              

                
NameTypeDefaultDescription

                
                  
variant*'filled' | 'outlined' | 'elevated' | 'text' | 'icon-only''filled'Visual treatment. icon-only hides the label and requires ariaLabel.

                  
labelstring''Rendered text content. Ignored for icon-only.

                  
iconstringundefinedGlyph or icon token rendered before the label.

                  
size'sm' | 'md' | 'lg''md'Height preset: 32 / 40 / 48px.

                  
disabledbooleanfalseRemoves pointer events, reduces opacity, sets aria-disabled.

NameEmitsDescription
